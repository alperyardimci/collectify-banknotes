Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2025

We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

Collectify Banknotes ("the App") is developed by Alper Yardimci. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

Collectify Banknotes does not collect any personal data. The App:

  • Does not require an account or registration
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not contain advertising or ad-tracking SDKs
  • Does not make network requests or connect to any servers
  • Does not access your location, contacts, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide

Data Storage

All data you create within the App is stored locally on your device only:

  • Banknote records (denomination, currency, year, notes) are stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
  • Photos you capture or select are stored in the App's local document directory on your device.
  • Language preference is stored locally on your device.

No data is transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

Camera and Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library solely to allow you to photograph your banknotes. These permissions are:

  • Only activated when you choose to take or select a photo
  • Used exclusively to save banknote images locally on your device
  • Never used to collect, transmit, or share your photos

Third-Party Services

Collectify Banknotes does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are no analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising networks, social media integrations, or cloud storage services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete individual banknotes and their associated photos within the App.
  • You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App from your device.
  • No residual data remains on any server after deletion, because no data was ever sent to a server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
