Collectify Banknotes — Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a banknote?

Navigate to a country, then tap the "+" button or "Add First Banknote" button. Fill in the denomination, take or select a photo, enter the year, and tap Save.

Can I edit a banknote after adding it?

Yes. Open any banknote and tap the edit icon (pencil) in the top right corner. You can update all fields including photos.

How do I delete a banknote?

Open the banknote detail screen and scroll down to tap "Delete". You'll be asked to confirm before the banknote is permanently removed.

Does the app require internet?

No. Collectify works 100% offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection is ever needed.

Where are my photos stored?

All photos are stored in the app's local storage on your device. They are not uploaded to any server or cloud service.

How do I switch between English and Turkish?

Tap the globe icon on the home screen to toggle between English and Turkish.

Will I lose my data if I update the app?

No. App updates preserve your existing data. However, uninstalling the app will delete all stored banknotes and photos.

How do I back up my collection?

Your collection is included in your standard iPhone backup (iCloud or computer backup). Restoring your device from a backup will also restore your Collectify data.
