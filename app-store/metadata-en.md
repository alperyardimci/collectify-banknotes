## Description (4000 characters max)
Collectify Banknotes is the ultimate companion for paper currency collectors. Whether you're a seasoned numismatist or just starting your collection, Collectify helps you organize, track, and showcase every banknote you own.

BROWSE THE WORLD
Explore 195 countries organized across 6 continents — Africa, Asia, Europe, North America, South America, and Oceania. Each country displays its flag, currency code, and your collection progress at a glance.

DOCUMENT EVERY BANKNOTE
Add banknotes with full details:
- Denomination and currency
- Front and back photos (snap with camera or pick from gallery)
- Circulation year range
- Mark if still in current circulation
- Personal notes and observations

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
Watch your collection grow with visual progress bars for each continent. See how many countries you've collected from, discover which regions are still waiting for you, and view your total banknote count on the home screen.

BEAUTIFULLY ORGANIZED
Your collection is neatly sorted into "Your Collection" and "Undiscovered" sections within each continent. The elegant dark theme with gold accents puts the spotlight on your banknotes.

BILINGUAL SUPPORT
Switch between English and Turkish with a single tap. All 195 country names, continent names, and interface text are fully translated.

100% OFFLINE & PRIVATE
Collectify works entirely without an internet connection. All your data — banknote details, photos, and preferences — stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud services, no tracking, no ads. Your collection is yours alone.

KEY FEATURES
- 195 countries across 6 continents
- Front and back banknote photography
- Visual collection progress tracking
- Year range and circulation status
- Personal notes for each banknote
- English and Turkish language support
- Elegant dark theme with gold accents
- Haptic feedback for a premium feel
- Completely offline — no internet needed
- Zero data collection — 100% private

Start your global banknote collection journey today with Collectify.
